Vision

The vision of the project is revive the lost art of bespoke tailoring by taking advantage of the technology currently available and the growing number of online shoppers in the country. The project’s intention is for customers to be able to custom order suits with minimum input from the company’s tailors. They intend to handle the majority of the orders via the web application and be able to take requests from all over the country and shipped the finished suits to their customers.
